Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services and procedures.

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine checkup and cleaning. However, some patients with specific conditions may need to visit more frequently. Your dentist will create a personalized schedule based on your oral health needs.

Coverage depends on your specific insurance plan. We accept most major dental insurance providers and will gladly help you understand your benefits. Our team will submit claims on your behalf and work to maximize your coverage.

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. For more extensive treatments, we offer flexible payment plans through third-party financing options like CareCredit. We'll work with you to find a payment solution that fits your budget.

We understand dental anxiety is common. Our team is trained to help you feel comfortable through gentle techniques, clear communication, and a calming environment. We also offer sedation options for patients who need extra relaxation during treatment.

For severe pain, knocked-out teeth, or other urgent dental issues, call us immediately at (555) 123-4567. We reserve time in our schedule each day for emergency patients. If it's after hours, follow the instructions on our voicemail to reach the on-call dentist.
